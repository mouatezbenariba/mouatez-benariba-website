Case Study Elzero Frontend Challenges

Overview

Elzero Frontend Challenges is a collection of coding challenges designed for learners who want to improve their HTML and CSS skills. Created by Osama Mohamed (Elzero), these challenges are available in the frontend track of Elzero Web School.

Each challenge presents a design or image that the learner needs to recreate using HTML and CSS. The aim is to achieve a perfect pixel match to the original design.

I have compiled a list of the Elzero Frontend Challenges and their corresponding descriptions on my Github account. This provides a clear understanding of what each challenge entails and the skills required to complete them.

Having this information easily accessible in one location can help anyone looking to complete the challenges keep track of their progress and ensure they meet the expectations set for each challenge. The challenges themselves can be accessed from here .

What is Elzero Web School?

Elzero Web School is an online educational platform that offers a variety of courses and tutorials on web development and design. The courses cover a range of topics, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and more. The platform is known for its engaging teaching style and practical approach to learning, with many courses offering hands-on assignments and real-world projects. Elzero Web School has a large community of students and developers who share knowledge, provide feedback, and collaborate on projects. It is a popular resource for anyone looking to improve their skills in web development and design.
